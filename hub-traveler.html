— Why this hub

なぜ、観光ゴルフを"福岡"でやるのか

福岡空港の都心アクセス、玄界灘・筑紫平野の地形、太宰府・宗像という1300年級の文化遺産。観光ゴルフに必要な3要素が、半径50km圏内に揃う稀有なエリアです。

🌊
海・山・川の景観が近い
玄界灘の真上に広がる芥屋GC、糸島湾を望むクイーンズヒル、筑後川と耳納連山の久留米CC。コース内から景色を楽しむ"観光ゴルフ"の本領を発揮できます。
⛩️
文化体験とセット
太宰府天満宮、宗像大社、太宰府門前町。コース近くに1300年級の文化遺産があるのは、世界的にも極めて稀。ラウンド前後に立ち寄るだけで「ただのゴルフ」が「日本旅行」になります。
📅
ベストシーズンが長い
3〜5月の桜・新緑、9〜11月の紅葉・好天が観光ゴルフの黄金期。台風シーズン(8〜9月上旬)と真夏の暑さ以外は、ほぼ1年中"観光と組み合わせて気持ちよく回れる"気候です。

— Why this hub

Why pair sightseeing golf with Fukuoka?

Fukuoka Airport's near-downtown location, the contrast between the Genkai Sea coast and the Chikushi plain, and 1,300-year-old shrines like Dazaifu and Munakata — all within a 50 km radius. A rare combination for sightseeing golf.

🌊
Sea, mountains, rivers — all close
Keya Golf Club sits directly above the Genkai Sea; Queens Hill looks over Itoshima Bay; Kurume CC frames the Chikugo River and Mino mountains. Scenery from the tee, not just on the way there.
⛩️
Cultural sites within easy reach
Dazaifu Tenmangu, Munakata Taisha, the Dazaifu shrine town. Having UNESCO-grade heritage so close to the courses is internationally rare. A quick stop before or after turns "just golf" into "Japan travel."
📅
A long best-season window
Cherry blossoms and fresh greenery in March–May; clear autumn weather and foliage in October–November. Outside of typhoon season (late August) and midsummer heat, almost any week works.

1
Itoshima · Genkai-nada Coast · Seaside
🌊 Keya Golf Club
Genkai-Sea view Itoshima Best for sightseeing 18H · Par 72
A links-style course on the Itoshima peninsula, set directly above the Genkai Sea. The downhill horizon shots are some of Fukuoka's most striking and a quiet pilgrimage site for international golfers. Pair the round with Itoshima's seaside cafes, oyster huts, and the Sakurai Futamigaura "married rocks" for a textbook Fukuoka day.
From ¥10,000–22,000

Dazaifu Tenmangu pairing Forest 18H
A forest course adjacent to Dazaifu Tenmangu, the shrine of learning. After the round, the approach path delivers umegae-mochi rice cakes, the Kyushu National Museum, and the famously sculptural Starbucks Dazaifu store. 30 minutes from FUK Airport — the canonical first-trip pairing.
From ¥7,000–14,000

A heritage parkland course overlooking the Chikugo River and Mino mountains. Pair it with Chikugogawa or Harazuru hot-spring inns for the canonical "round → onsen → Kurume ramen" overnight. About one hour from FUK — easy first visit from Tokyo or Kansai.

A scenic resort-style course on the Itoshima hillside. Many holes overlook Itoshima Bay and the Genkai Sea, with afternoon tee-times catching the sunset. Pair the round with Shiraito Falls, the photogenic Itoshima cafes, and Sakurai Futamigaura for the most Instagram-friendly day in Fukuoka.

왜 "후쿠오카"에서 관광 골프인가

후쿠오카공항의 도심 접근성, 겐카이나다·치쿠시평야의 지형, 다자이후·무나카타라는 1300년급 문화유산. 관광 골프에 필요한 3요소가 반경 50km 안에 모두 모인 드문 에리어입니다.

🌊
바다·산·강의 풍경이 가깝다
겐카이나다 위에 펼쳐지는 게야GC, 이토시마만을 바라보는 퀸즈힐, 치쿠고가와와 미노 산맥의 구루메CC. 코스 안에서 풍경을 즐기는 "관광 골프"의 진면목.
⛩️
문화 체험과 세트
다자이후 텐만구, 무나카타 대사, 다자이후 몬젠마치. 코스 가까이에 1300년급 문화유산이 있는 것은 세계적으로도 매우 드문 일. 라운드 전후에 들르기만 해도 "단순 골프"가 "일본 여행"이 됩니다.
📅
베스트 시즌이 길다
3〜5월 벚꽃·신록, 9〜11월 단풍·맑은 날씨가 관광 골프의 황금기. 태풍 시즌(8월 말〜9월 초)과 한여름 더위 외에는 거의 1년 내내 "관광과 함께 기분 좋게 도는" 기후입니다.
