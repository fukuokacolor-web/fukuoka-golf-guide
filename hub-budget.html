— Saving Tactics

¥3,000節約する、4つの実践テク

"なんとなく週末"を卒業するだけで、年間ラウンド数が1.5倍になります。明日から使えるテクニックを4つ。

1
月曜・火曜・木曜を狙う
水曜定休のコースが多いため、火曜と木曜は人気が分散します。月曜は"セルフ¥7,880"のような特別プランも出やすい曜日。週末より¥2,500〜¥4,000安くなるのが標準。
2
午後スルーは"通の選択"
12時前後スタート→ハーフ昼食を取らずに18Hを通す形式。料金は午前スタートより¥1,500〜¥2,500安い。プレー時間も4時間程度に短縮されるので、家族時間とも両立しやすい。
3
Web予約サイト2社を比較
同じコース・同じ日付でも、じゃらん・楽天GORAで¥500〜¥2,000差が出るケースが多数。両方検索が"基本動作"。Pontaか楽天、貯めているポイントの方を優先するとさらにお得。
4
季節ごとの価格差を狙う
福岡は1月・2月・8月が底値(¥4,060〜の枠が出やすい)、3〜5月・10〜11月がピーク。寒さを我慢する1〜2月、暑さを我慢する8月のラウンドはコスパ最強。レイヤリングで快適に。

— Why this hub

Same budget, double the rounds.

One ¥15,000 round per month progresses your game less than two ¥7,500 rounds. This hub is the price/time/booking combination that makes that math work.

📅
Weekday tee times save ¥3,000
Most Fukuoka clubs run ¥2,500–¥4,000 cheaper Monday–Friday than on weekends. If you can spend a vacation day on a Tuesday round, you free up over ¥30,000/year — easily another two rounds.
🌤️
Afternoon-thru: ¥1,500–¥2,500 off
Tee off around noon, skip the long mid-round lunch, finish 18 by 5pm. Many clubs price afternoon-thru ¥1,500–¥2,500 below the standard morning round, and you still get home for dinner.
🎯
Jalan / Rakuten GORA reward points
Jalan Golf earns Ponta points; Rakuten GORA earns Rakuten points. Web-only deals are common, and the same course can be ¥1,000–¥2,000 cheaper than at the front desk on any given day.
— Saving Tactics

Four tactics to save ¥3,000/round

Stop defaulting to "weekend." Four small habit shifts that translate to roughly 1.5x more rounds per year.

1
Aim for Mon / Tue / Thu
Many clubs close on Wednesdays, so Tuesday and Thursday demand is diluted. Mondays often carry "self-play ¥7,880"-class specials. Expect ¥2,500–¥4,000 below weekend rates.
2
Afternoon-thru is the insider play
Around noon start → 18 holes without the long lunch break. ¥1,500–¥2,500 cheaper than morning rounds, and play wraps in roughly four hours. Family-friendly Saturdays become possible.
3
Compare two web sites
For the same course on the same day, Jalan Golf and Rakuten GORA can differ ¥500–¥2,000. Checking both should be reflex. Pick the side where you bank points already.
4
Ride the seasonal floor
In Fukuoka, January, February, and August are the price floor (¥4,060 slots appear). March–May and October–November are peak. Layer up in winter, hydrate in August — the savings are real.

"그냥 주말"을 졸업하는 것만으로 연간 라운드 수가 1.5배. 내일부터 쓸 수 있는 4가지.

2
오후 스루는 "고수의 선택"
12시 전후 시작→하프 점심을 거르고 18H를 통과. 요금은 오전 시작보다 ¥1,500〜¥2,500 저렴. 플레이 시간도 4시간 정도로 단축되어 가족 시간과도 양립.
3
웹 예약 사이트 2곳 비교
같은 코스·같은 날짜라도 자란·라쿠텐 GORA에서 ¥500〜¥2,000 차이가 나는 경우가 다수. 양쪽 검색이 "기본 동작". 폰타나 라쿠텐, 적립 중인 포인트 쪽을 우선하면 더 이득.
