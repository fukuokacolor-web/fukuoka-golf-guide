⛳Moon Lake Golf Club — Kurate Course
〒807-1308　1331 Yahiro, Kurate-machi, Kurate-gun, Fukuoka
📞 0949-42-8200
Operated by: PGM (Pacific Golf Management)
💡Always verify the latest access information on the club's official website.
✈️ From Fukuoka Airport
✈️From Fukuoka Airport (Recommended Route)
  • Take Fukuoka Urban Expressway → Kyushu Expressway
  • Exit at Kurate IC
  • Approx. 9 min from the IC to the course
  • Total: approx. 60 min from Fukuoka Airport / Hakata by car
💡Search "Moon Lake Golf Club Kurate" in Google Maps or Naver Maps for the best route.

🚃Train + Taxi
The nearest station is JR Chikuho Main Line "Akamageki Station". There is no direct bus — take a taxi (approx. 25 min).
