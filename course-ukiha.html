— The Course

Why golfers love
this course.

Opened June 1997 in Niikawa, Ukiha-cho, Ukiha City. Supervised by pro Hideyo Sugimoto. An 18-hole hilly layout (Par 72, 6,729 yards). About 15 km / 18 minutes from Oita Expressway Haki IC — solid access plus excellent value for weekday early-morning rounds.

  • ✓
    Pro Hideyo Sugimoto's supervisionHill-country layout opened 1997. Par 72, 6,729Y.
